About Us

E Pe is a forward-thinking fintech brand under the umbrella of M/S Techfin Innovations Private Limited, based in Delhi, India. Incorporated on July 3, 2023, E Pe is designed to provide a wide array of financial services tailored to meet the needs of diverse customers across the nation. The platform offers services that span reserves management, various types of loans, investment opportunities, and personalized consultations, all through a user-friendly and innovative digital interface.

E Pe also differentiates itself by offering value-added services that go beyond basic financial products. For instance, customers have access to free startup and business consultation, covering areas like incorporation, compliance management, and strategic planning. This support is particularly beneficial for entrepreneurs and small businesses looking to establish or grow their operations with expert guidance. In addition, E Pe provides career and investment counseling through partnerships with experienced professionals, helping customers make informed decisions about their future and their finances.
